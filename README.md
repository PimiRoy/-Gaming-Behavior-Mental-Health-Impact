Project Title

Job in AI Era: Future of Work & Skills Transformation

![Job in AI Era](https://github.com/PimiRoy/-Job-in-AI-Era-And--Impact/blob/main/AI%20Era.png)

Objective

This project analyzes how Artificial Intelligence is reshaping the job market and identifies the skills required to succeed in the evolving digital economy.

Industry Relevance

This study is relevant to:

IT & Data Industry
Business Analytics
AI & Machine Learning Sector
Corporate Workforce Planning
Problem Statement

With rapid AI adoption, many traditional jobs are changing. The challenge is to understand:

Which jobs are at risk
Which new roles are emerging
What skills are required for future careers
Key Responsibilities / Analysis Areas
Study impact of AI on employment trends
Identify high-demand technical skills
Analyze shift from manual to AI-driven work
Explore future job roles in AI ecosystem
Suggest upskilling roadmap for students

Skills & Tools Used

Power BI 📈
Data Analysis 📉

Key Findings

AI is automating repetitive tasks faster than expected
Demand for data and AI-related roles is increasing
Hybrid skills (technical + business) are highly valuable
Continuous learning is required for job security

Future Job Roles in AI Era
Data Analyst 📊
AI Engineer 🤖
Machine Learning Engineer
Prompt Engineer
Business Intelligence Analyst

Project Outcome
This project helps in understanding:

Future job market direction
Skill gap in current education system
Career planning in AI-driven economy
